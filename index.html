Story

Founded by Chaou.B and joined by Jadigg. Rhytmics is their peacefull land where magic always happen. They are throwing alternativ parties all around Morocco with the very best artists from the international underground music scene.

DJ's

Chaou.B

The Moroccan DJ is the mastermind behind one of the most alternative party brands called « Rhytmics ». He invited renowned international guest to his party in Morocco like Suciu, Okain, Jessica Diaz, Andy Kolwes, DoubtingThomas, Franco Cinelli, Livia, Alex (Voiceless), Julian Alexander, and many others.

Jadigg

Jade Benzakour, alias Jadigg, is a young music electronic lover from Morocco.

Ja:ad

Ja:ad is a space traveller between deep minimalistic sound, crossing the big house and techno wall that will lead you directly to the dance floor.
